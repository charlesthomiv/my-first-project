Jared Eisenhauer

Where Soul Meets Americana

Based: Central Oregon From: Milwaukee, WI Performs: Solo, Duo, Full Band

About

From Milwaukee dive bars to Central Oregon's sun-soaked stages, Jared Eisenhauer has spent years crafting a sound that refuses to sit still. His music pulls from RnB grooves, Americana storytelling, and pop hooks — sometimes all in the same song.

Whether performing solo with a guitar, as a duo, or with a full band, Jared brings music that makes you feel something.
