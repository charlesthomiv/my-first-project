About

From Milwaukee dive bars to Central Oregon's sun-soaked stages, Jared Eisenhauer has spent years crafting a sound that refuses to sit still. His music pulls from RnB grooves, Americana storytelling, and pop hooks — sometimes all in the same song.

He's shared stages with Jeshua Marshall & the Flood, Joe Martin & The Electric Street Sign, Amargoso, Mason James of Double Jump, and Miguel Gonzalez — and whether he's performing solo with a guitar, as a duo, or with a full band, he brings the same energy: music that makes you feel something.
