About

Jared Eisenhauer is a successful singer & songwriter from Milwaukee, WI, now based in Central Oregon. Known for his catchy mix of original RnB, Americana & pop music, Jared has graced stages from Portland, OR to Detroit, MI.

He's opened for and performed with notable artists including Jeshua Marshall & the Flood, Joe Martin & The Electric Street Sign, Amargoso, Mason James of Double Jump, and Miguel Gonzalez.
